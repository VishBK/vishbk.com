Music

I started seriously listening to music in 2013 and rap quickly became my favorite genre. Now I listen to a lot of artists and would like to share my recommendations, if you value my opinion. All of these albums/songs are at least an 8/10 for me.

I started to track what tracks I listened to using Last.fm in December 2018. I created this Tableau project to visualize my top tracks, albums, and artists over any period of time. There's also other interesting things such as what time of day I listen to the most music and the number of new artists I've listened to every month. These graphs are interactive, so feel free to change the filters and click around!

Please note: The Last.fm app missed many of my listens on my phone when I started this in December 2018, and I only found out and fixed it in July 2019.
